# fire-detection
Intelligent fire detection and control center

🔥 FireVision
Real-Time Fire & Heat Detection Using Your Camera
📌 What Is FireVision?
FireVision is a web-based software that uses your CCTV camera or webcam to detect fire or extreme heat in real-time.
As soon as it detects a flame or abnormal heat anywhere in the frame, it immediately sends an alert to your mobile phone—no expensive sensors required.
All you need is a camera, an old laptop or smartphone, and this software.

🧩 What Problem Does It Solve?
Traditional fire alarm systems (smoke or heat detectors) have two major flaws:

Issue	Explanation
💰 Expensive	They require professional installation and high-cost equipment.
⏳ Slow Response	They only activate after smoke or excessive heat has already spread—often too late.
FireVision solves these problems by seeing the fire before it grows.
It detects:

A small spark behind electrical wiring ⚡

Overheating of a device or machine 🔥

Early flames in a kitchen or workshop 🍳

As soon as it sees the first sign, it sends an instant alert to your phone—giving you time to act before disaster strikes.

🏠 Where Can You Use It?
FireVision is highly flexible and works with any standard camera. You can monitor:

Location	Use Case
🏭 Factories & Warehouses	Detect overheating machines or electrical faults
🏠 Homes & Apartments	Kitchen safety, room monitoring
🏗️ Construction Sites	Watch for fire hazards in temporary setups
🏫 Schools & Offices	Early fire warning for large buildings
🌲 Forests & Farms	Detect wildfires before they spread
🚗 Parking Garages	Vehicle fire detection

🔧 How Does It Work?
Camera captures live video

AI analyzes frames for flame or abnormal heat patterns

If detected, an alert is sent to your phone (via SMS, push notification, or app)

You receive the warning instantly, giving you a critical head start
